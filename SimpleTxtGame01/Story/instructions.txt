Here are the basic commands that you will need to know to traverse the world of STG:

LOOK:           Describes to you the current location that you are in.
MOVE(N/S/E/W):  Through these commands you can choose to move in any direction, north, south, east or west provided that the area is not restricted.
ATTACK:         Allows you to attack enemies if you are in combat.
TALK(1/2/3/4):  Can choose to talk to other NPCs using selected dialogue.
INVENTORY:      Through this you can access your inventory of items on your person.
SAVE:           Saves data at your current location.
QUIT:           Saves and quits the game.
HELP:           Allows you to view all possible commands again.
